Engineering Philosophy

TiMELiNES is designed as an institutional historical publishing platform rather than a traditional content management system.

The platform separates:

* historical authority;
* governance;
* publication;
* presentation;
* operational execution.

Every engineering milestone was implemented under this institutional model.
