# Research Pipeline

Authority Level: Factory Architecture
Governed System: Factory research and evidence doctrine.
Describes: Both

## Scope
This document governs Factory research language, evidence ownership, and Tiered Source Authority alignment.

## Non-Scope
This document does not select research providers, define data storage, or prescribe implementation workflow.

## Product Identity
Factory research supports a Chronological Knowledge Platform by gathering evidence for Historical Objects, Milestones, relationships, and Timeline Views.

## Historical Object Doctrine
Research may identify Historical Objects: Person, Institution, Place, Technology, Publication, Conflict, Movement, and Period.

## Milestone Doctrine
Research must support Milestones as canonical chronological knowledge units. Research should not treat Timeline Views as the primary evidence container.

## Timeline View Doctrine
Research may support Timeline Views, but Timeline Views are narrative assemblies derived from Milestones and Historical Objects.

## Tiered Source Authority
Research is governed by Tiered Source Authority:
- Sources provide evidence.
- Publishers provide evidence authority.
- Source tier must be distinguishable from source existence.
- Evidence strength must be preserved for validation and Publication Package review.

## Provenance Doctrine
Research artifacts must preserve enough provenance to support source tier assessment, Milestone validation, and Feedback Package review.
