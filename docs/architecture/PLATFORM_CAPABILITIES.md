## Purpose

This document inventories repository-backed platform capabilities and distinguishes certified institutional architecture from production launch work and future evolution.

---

## Platform Identity

TiMELiNES is a Chronological Knowledge Platform.

The platform organizes, preserves, publishes, and explores historical knowledge through chronology while preserving authority, context, governance, provenance, and institutional memory.

---

## Capability Maturity Scale

Certified: implemented, tested, and covered by institutional certification.

Locked: protected by constitutional or architectural doctrine.

Active: current production-launch work.

Planned: future authorized work.

Future: not current launch scope.
